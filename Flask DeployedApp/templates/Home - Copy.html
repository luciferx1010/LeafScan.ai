Our Technologies

Machine Learning Algorithms

Various machine learning algorithms such as Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), Support Vector Machines (SVMs), or Random Forests are utilized for image classification and disease detection.

Image Processing

Techniques such as image segmentation, feature extraction, and image augmentation are employed to preprocess and enhance the quality of plant images before feeding them into the machine learning models.

Deep Learning Frameworks

Frameworks like TensorFlow, PyTorch, or Keras are commonly used to build, train, and deploy complex deep learning models for accurate disease detection based on plant images.

Hyperpectral Imaging

Hyperpectral imaging technology involves capturing images of plants across multiple wavelengths, providing detailed spectral information for each pixel.
